Game Rules

Goal

Save your balls! If it's your turn and you have no balls left, you lose.

Setup

Each player gets 15 balls. The board is a 4×4 grid where you build a pyramid together.

Placing a Ball

  • On your turn, place one of your balls on any open spot.
  • You can place on a higher level once all four balls below it are filled.

Raising a Ball

  • Instead of placing a new ball, you can move one of your balls that's already on the board to a higher level.
  • You can only move a ball if nothing is on top of it.
  • This saves you a ball — very useful!

Completing a Square

  • If four of your balls form a 2×2 square, you may take back 1 or 2 of your balls from the board.
  • You can only take back balls with nothing on top of them.
  • This is optional — you can skip it.

Winning

You win by placing the top ball of the pyramid, or if your opponent runs out of balls. Try to raise balls and complete squares to keep your supply up!
